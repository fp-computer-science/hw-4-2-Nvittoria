Fairfield Prep

Fairfield Prep is a Jesuit High School in Fairfield, Connecticut.
The school is an all boys private school that excels in academics,
sports, and faith. over the course of its almost 80 year history,
Fairfield Prep has been a school of excelence. The boys of Fairfield
Prep are all brothers learning together to go out in the world and make
God's world a better place.

Athletics

Fairfield prep has one of the best athletic programs in the state, and it
has shaped young men to learn the balance of being a student athlete. Fairfield Prep
athletics has also created bonds between classmates that will last forever.
